Delay Timer Circuit

Door Status Detector

The opening and closing of doors is detected using the Hall Effect sensor. A Hall effect sensor is a device that is used to measure the magnitude of a magnetic field. Its output voltage is directly proportional to the magnetic field strength through it. Hall effect sensors are used for proximity sensing, positioning, speed detection, and current sensing applications. Bringing a strong magnet or magnetic field towards the sensor would lead to the output pin to toggle, efficiently triggering the ON and OFF state of the lights. The output pins of the microcontroller is being latched here. This particular latch will remember a digital High reading at the output of the sensor until an opposing magnetic field is sensed, at which point it will drop to digital Low and stay there until it is triggered again.

Master Slave Bot

Swarm robotics is a new approach to the coordination of multi-robot systems which consist of large numbers of mostly simple physical robots. This approach emerged in the field of artificial swarm intelligence as well as the biological study of insects, ants and other fields in the nature, where a swarm behaviour occurs. Swarm Robotics is an emerging area in collective robotics which uses a fully distributed control paradigm and relatively simple robots to achieve coordinated behaviour at the group level. In Swarm Robotics, we through manual or autonomous way, control the working of the principal bot. The principal bot in turn controls all the remaining slave bots connected to it either through wireless medium. All the processes and paths followed by the master bot is invariantly passed on to the slave bot(s), which all are bound to follow the leader.
